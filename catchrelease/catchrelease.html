FAQs

Find answers to common questions about catch and release fishing.

What should I bring?

It is recommended to bring sunscreen, a hat, sun glasses, comfortable clothing, and any personal item you may need.
We provide all fishing equipment

How long is the trip?

The duration of the catch and release fishing depends on the package you selected.
We offer half day and full day packages to accomodate your preferences.

What types of fish can I expect?

The types of fish you can expext during the catch and release fishing trip vary depending on the location and season.
Common species include bass, trout, salmon and catfish.

Can I keep the fish

No, catch and release fishing is focused on preserving the fish population. After catching a fish, it will be released back into the water

Is fishing experience required?

No prior fishing experience is required for our catch and release fishing trips. Our experienced guides will provide instruction and assistance throughout the trip.
